WHAT WE OFFER



Formula 1 offers much more than just a race day excitement—— It provides a rich experience that captivates audiences on multiple levels. Not only that, Formula 1 also offers multi-deminesional experience to the public, captirung the range of excitement, innovation, and engagement that fans and spectators will surely enjoy. Here's what F1 offers to the public:







GLOBAL SPECTACLE

With races held in glamorous cities like Monaco, Singapore, and Abu Dhabi, F1 offers a front-row seat to global destinations. The sport’s glamorous image is as much about the locations, celebrity appearances, and VIP lifestyle as it is about the racing.

THRILLING ENTERTAINMENT

At its core, F1 is a pulse-pounding sporting event. Fans witness 20 of the world’s best drivers competing at breakneck speeds, with races often decided by fractions of a second. The drama of wheel-to-wheel battles, overtakes, and crashes keeps spectators on the edge of their seats.

For the public, F1 provides a range of exclusive experiences—from paddock tours and meet-and-greets with drivers to premium hospitality packages at race weekends. Fans can also dive deep into the sport with behind-the-scenes content, team radio broadcasts, and in-depth analysis provided by F1 TV and social media platforms.
